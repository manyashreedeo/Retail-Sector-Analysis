# Retail Sector Analysis

## Overview

This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data to derive actionable business insights. The primary objective is to clean and standardize raw transactional data and perform exploratory and descriptive analysis to understand sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends.The analysis supports data-driven decision-making for sales optimization and customer segmentation.

## Objectives

* Clean and prepare retail transaction data for accurate analysis
* Analyze sales and quantity performance at product and category levels
* Understand customer purchasing behavior and transaction patterns
* Identify top-performing and underperforming products
* Examine sales trends and revenue patterns over time

## Scope of Work

The analysis covers the end-to-end data preparation and summarization process, including data cleaning, aggregation, and trend analysis, with a focus on business-relevant metrics.

## Data Cleaning & Preparation

The following data quality issues were identified and resolved:

* Removal of duplicate transaction records
* Correction of incorrect or inconsistent product prices
* Identification and replacement of null or missing values
* Standardization of data formats for consistency

## Analysis Performed

* Total sales value and quantity sold per product
* Number of transactions made by each customer
* Performance analysis of product categories
* Identification of top 10 best-selling and bottom 10 least-selling products
* Sales trend and revenue pattern analysis over time
* Total amount spent by each customer
* Customer segmentation based on the total number of products purchased

## Key Insights

* Clear visibility into high-performing and low-performing products
* Identification of valuable customers based on spending and purchase volume
* Understanding of category-level contribution to overall revenue
* Detection of sales trends that can support inventory and pricing decisions

## Business Impact

* Enables informed pricing and inventory management decisions
* Helps identify loyal and high-value customers for targeted marketing
* Supports strategic planning through trend and performance analysis
* Improves data reliability through structured data cleaning

## Tech Stack

* SQL (for data cleaning and analysis)

## How to Use

1. Load the retail dataset into the database or analysis environment
2. Execute the data cleaning queries/scripts
3. Run analytical queries to generate summaries and insights
4. Review results and visualizations for interpretation
